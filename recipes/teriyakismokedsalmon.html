Teriyaki Smoked Salmon

About This Recipe

This is an UPDATED Teriyaki Smoked Salmon recipe - easy to prepare with just a few ingredients!

Recipe Details

  • Prep: PT10M
  • Cook: PT2H
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp fresh grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 Salmon Portions (about 2 lbs.)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl mix together the brown sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, and honey. Whisk until combined.
  2. Brush the tops and sides of the salmon with the teriyaki glaze.
  3. Transfer the salmon to the smoker and place on your grill grate skin side down, and smoke at 200째 F for 1 hour.
  4. After the hour, brush the salmon with the remaining glaze.
  5. Continue to smoke until your desired internal temperature is reaches. I prefer 135-140째F. (approximately 2 hours total)
  6. When finished, pull the salmon off and enjoy warm.
